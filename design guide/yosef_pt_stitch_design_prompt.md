GOOGLE STITCH / AI DESIGN TOOL PROMPT
======================================
Use this prompt verbatim in Google Stitch, Galileo AI, Uizard, or similar UI generation tools.


PROMPT:
-------

Design a complete, multi-page premium personal trainer website called "Yosef Personaaltreener". The brand identity is athletic, modern, and high-end — inspired by a logo that uses deep navy blue, antique gold, and warm cream/ivory as its core colours.

COLOUR PALETTE (use exactly):
- Primary background: Deep navy #1B2A4A
- Secondary background: Warm cream #F5F0E8
- Accent / CTA colour: Antique gold #B8952A
- Text on dark: White #FFFFFF and cream #F5F0E8
- Text on light: Near-black #1A1A1A
- Muted text: Grey #6B7280
- Lighter gold for hovers: #D4AF5A

TYPOGRAPHY:
- All display headings and section titles: Bebas Neue font, always uppercase
- All body text, buttons, labels, captions: DM Sans font
- Heading sizes: Hero 96px, Page title 64px, Card title 32px
- Body size: 16px, button labels 15px bold

VISUAL DIRECTION:
Premium athletic brand aesthetic. Clean, structured, high-contrast. Navy and cream sections alternate throughout the page. Gold is only used for buttons, icons, borders, and accent lines — never as a section fill. Think high-end sports coaching brand, not commercial gym. Modern, spacious layout with generous white space. Subtle 3D geometric abstract element in the hero (gold wireframe rotating shape). No stock photo vibes. No purple, no gradients on text, no neon.

PAGES TO DESIGN (design all of these):

PAGE 1 — HOMEPAGE
- Full-height hero: navy background, large headline "TRAIN SMART. LIFT HEAVY. SEE RESULTS." in white Bebas Neue, two CTA buttons (gold filled + gold outlined), abstract 3D gold wireframe geometric shape floating on the right side
- Three feature cards on cream background: 1-to-1 Training Plans, Online Training Plans, Book a Consultation — each with an icon, short description, gold top-border accent, hover state
- About section: navy bg, coach circular photo placeholder left, name + bio + gold divider + stats right
- Testimonials: cream bg, 3 cards with 5 gold stars, quote, client initials in navy circle
- Bottom CTA banner: navy gradient background, large centred headline, single gold button

PAGE 2 — 1-TO-1 TRAINING PLANS PAGE
- Compact hero banner (40vh), navy, bold heading
- Gold-bordered notice: "Plan content is delivered privately after purchase"
- 3 plan cards in a row: Starter, Transformation (middle card highlighted with gold border + "Most Popular" badge), Elite
- Each card: plan name in Bebas Neue, tagline, duration/sessions row, features list with gold checkmarks, large "TBD" price placeholder, full-width gold CTA button

PAGE 3 — ONLINE TRAINING PLANS + STEPPER FORM
- Short hero banner
- Multi-step form on navy background:
  - Step 1: Goal selection (4 large radio cards with icons)
  - Step 2: Experience level (4 radio cards)
  - Step 3: Days available (3 radio cards)
  - Step 4: Equipment access (4 multi-select checkbox cards)
  - Step 5: Recommendation result — one large featured plan card + 2 smaller alternative cards
- Gold animated progress bar at top of form
- Selected card state: gold border + gold left strip + gold tinted background
- Back/Next navigation buttons

PAGE 4 — BOOK A CONSULTATION PAGE
- Hero: "BOOK A 1-TO-1 SESSION" headline
- 3 icon + text cards: Video Call, Personalised Advice, Action Plan
- Large centred price display: "Session Price" label + "TBD" in large gold Bebas Neue
- 3-step "How it works" numbered process list
- Large gold CTA button: "PAY & BOOK YOUR SESSION"

PAGE 5 — BOOKING CONFIRMED PAGE
- Success confirmation banner (gold checkmark icon + green tint)
- Calendar embed placeholder area (large box with dashed border)
- Short instruction text

PAGE 6 — PAYMENT SUCCESS
- Centred layout, navy background
- Large animated gold checkmark
- "PAYMENT CONFIRMED" heading
- Short confirmation message
- "Back to Home" button

PAGE 7 — PAYMENT CANCELLED
- Centred layout, cream background
- Outlined X icon
- "PAYMENT CANCELLED" heading
- Short reassurance message
- "Go Back" button

SHARED COMPONENTS:
- Navbar: logo "YOSEF PT" in gold Bebas Neue + small star icon, transparent on hero then navy on scroll, links: Plans (dropdown), Book a Session, mobile hamburger
- Footer: navy, logo + tagline, nav links, social icons (Instagram, WhatsApp), copyright line

INTERACTION STATES TO SHOW:
- Button hover: gold shimmer
- Card hover: slight lift + deeper shadow
- Selected stepper option: gold border + gold background tint
- Disabled button (price TBD): greyed out, "Price coming soon" badge

Please design all pages with consistent spacing, component reuse, and the same visual language throughout. The result should feel like one cohesive premium brand, not a collection of individual screens.
